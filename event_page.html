Book Festival

The National Book Trust, in partnership with the Pune Municipal Corporation,hosted a festival at Fergusson College from December 16 to 24. We had the pleasure of attending the event on December 17, 2023. The atmosphere was vibrant and the crowd was impressive. It was heartening to see such a largegathering of book enthusiasts.Their passion for literature is truly commendable.
